      Methodology

      How OmniHub turns noise into shippable epics

      A look under the hood — the production data sources, pipeline stages, and the guarantees we ship with the MVP. The prototype renders synthetic data; the architecture below is what we'll wire up for live customers.

      Pipeline architecture

      § 01
      Ingest
      Normalize, dedupe, sentiment-score every signal at the edge.
      § 02
      Cluster
      Embeddings + LLM theme extraction; 60s rolling window.
      § 03
      Score
      RICE++ on every cluster — explainable, deterministic.
      § 04
      Recommend
      Top-1 epic with rationale, evidence, acceptance criteria.
      § 05
      Sync
      Jira epic + Confluence PRD draft, with linked references.
